Our Mission

Inspired by the collaboration and caffeine shared over coding projects, the creators of Code_lab Coffee decided venture into the world of coffee by creating a harmonious relationship with smaller, net-zero emission farms located in upstate New York. Roasted on-site at the flagship store in historic Bryant Park, their beans rich flavors create delicious drinks that inspire our patrons, served in 100% recycled and biodegradable cups and flatware.


Our Process - Take a peak inside!

Code_lab Coffee works exclusively with local, farmer owned coffee farms. Our 2016 beans have come from Daniel & Son Farms. Through our partnership, our seed-to-cup process has a carbon-neutral footprint.

Selectively picked by our farmers, the beans go through a fermenting and drying process down to 11% moisture. Once they reach the perfect moisture level, the beans are milled to remove the outer husk and sorted by size. Code_lab Coffee work with our partnered farms to hand select the best beans for our coffee flavors.

At this point, they beans are now referred to as green coffee. Roasting is the process that turns the green coffee into the aromatic brown beans. Our on-site roasting system keeps the beans in constant motion, preventing any burning or disruption of flavor. Our close relationships with our farms provide us with a constantly changing seasonal flavor collection, giving our patrons new options every time they visit.
